Online Payments Fraud Detection

A machine learning based intelligent system to detect fraudulent online transactions. This system analyzes customer behavior and transaction patterns to prevent financial loss.

About Online Payment Frauds

Online payment frauds occur when unauthorized or illegal transactions are carried out using digital payment systems such as UPI, debit cards, credit cards, and online banking. Fraudsters often exploit weak security, stolen credentials, or loopholes in payment systems to transfer money illegally.

With the rapid growth of digital transactions, fraud detection systems powered by Machine Learning have become essential. These systems analyze transaction patterns, user behavior, and balance changes to identify suspicious activities in real time and help prevent financial losses.
